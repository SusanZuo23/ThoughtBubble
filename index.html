Our Mission

At ThoughtBubble, we empower young minds to navigate a world full of psychological misinformation by providing an accessible space to learn science-based psychology. In an age where social media and brands often exploit how the brain works to influence behavior, we aim to equip people with the tools to recognize manipulation, think critically, and understand how their minds function. By grounding our content in real science, we help build awareness and informed decision-making for all of our members and listeners.
